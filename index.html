PolyRunner

is an endless runner game for virtual reality devices,
played as a damaged alien spaceship escaping across the desert near Area 51.

Created using Unity, Visual Studio, C#, and Oculus SDK, in a team of four.
Programmed player controls, ship shields, ship fuel drain and pickup, and tiling sand layers.

The Adventures of Rob & Ots

is a 2.5D online cooperative puzzle-platformer game,
played by two, using Rob & Ots unique abilities to work together and solve puzzles.

Created using Unity, Visual Studio, and C#, in a team of four.
Programmed detached camera controls, ping communications, sprint effect, and character selection.

Dolphin Flip

is a 2D launcher game for mobile devices,
played as a dolphin with dreams of the stars and rocket fuel to spare.

Created using Unity, Visual Studio, and C#, in a team of four.
Programmed the player controls, interactables and obstacles.

Granny Theft Auto
