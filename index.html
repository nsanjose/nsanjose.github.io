Portfolio

The Original Mobile Games

is a digitally recreated collection of handheld, dexterity games and puzzles, on the Android, iOS, and Nintendo Switch platforms.

  • Created in a partnership between the Strong Museum of Play, Second Avenue Learning, and Rochester Institute of Technology.
  • Worked on porting ‘The Original Mobile Games’ (TOMG) to the Nintendo Switch platform, and updates, optimizations, and bug fixes for ‘TOMG’ on the Android and iOS platforms.

Project Lily Pad

is a serious geogame for improving spatial thinking in disaster resilience.

  • Created in a team of National Science Foundation (NSF) Research Experience for Undergraduate (REU) students and geogame developers.
  • Integrated geospatial data to recreate setting, programmed quest, linear dialogue, checkpoint systems, and user interface for map view, landmarkers, and quest log.

Ori Engine

is a space for exploring rendering techniques and game engine development.

  • Created using C++, DirectX 11, and HLSL, as a personal project.

Escape Roswell

is an endless runner game for virtual reality devices.
is played as a damaged alien spaceship fleeing across the desert.

  • Created using C#, Unity, and Oculus SDK, in a team of four.
  • Programmed player controls, ship shields, ship fuel drain, and pickups.

The Adventures of Rob & Ots

is a 2.5D, cooperative multiplayer, puzzle-platformer game.
is played by two, using Rob & Ots unique abilities to work together and solve puzzles.

  • Created using C# and Unity, in a team of four.
  • Programmed camera controls, ping communications, sprint effect, and character selection.
