The Adventures of Rob & Ots

is a 2.5D online cooperative platformer game,
played by two, as robots of different talents working together to solve puzzles.

Role: Network and Gameplay Programmer, in a team of 4
Course: Online Virtual Worlds and Simulations
Used: Unity, Visual Studio, C#
Description: Our goal for this game was to support players across a network, and create engaging cooperative gameplay. I programmed networking features such as a visual communication tool, character selection, and other bug-fixing.

PolyRunner

is an endless runner game for virtual reality devices,
played as a damaged alien spaceship escaping across the desert near Area 51.

Role: Gameplay Programmer, in a team of 4
Course: Casual Game Development
Used: Unity, Visual Studio, C#, Oculus SDK
Description: Our goal for this game was to learn about virtual reality's integration into gameplay and game engines. I worked on design and implementation of our systems for player controls and procedural level generation.

Dolphin Flip
