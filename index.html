DirectX 11 Graphics Engine

is a 2.5D endless side-scroller, made with our team's graphics engine

Team Size: 3
Tools: Visual Studio, C++, Direct3D 11, HLSL
Course: Foundations of Game Graphics Programming


This goal of this project was to learn about, by creating, the basics of a graphics engine. We started individually with model loading, textures, and lighting. Then we got together with a group of two or three other students and merged what we had to continue with new features. I continued with work on shadow mapping, transparency, and implementation of our level design while my teammates worked on normal mapping, post-processing, and particles. We would also then utilize our graphics features towards a game.

The Adventures of Rob & Ots

is a 2.5D online cooperative platformer,
played by two, as robots of different talents working together to solve puzzles

Team Size: 4
Tools: Unity, Visual Studio, C#
Course: Online Virtual Worlds and Simulations


Our goal with this project was to support players aross a network. Our team consisted of one artist, one level designer, and two programmers. As one of the programmers, I worked on user interface, and networking such as a visual communication system and the unique class selection by the networked players.
